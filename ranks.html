1 Tip to climb out of every rank in Valorant:

Iron to Bronze

  • Not shooting while moving
  • Understanding the Agents and Wheapons

Bronze to Silver

  • Crosshair placement
  • Movement

Silver to Gold

  • Positioning
  • Aiming

Gold to Platinum

  • Communication
  • Playing the same Agents

Platinum to Diamond

  • Team play
  • Gamesense

Diamond to Ascendend

  • Minimizing bad games you have and playing on a good level every round
  • Consistent Aim and not Tilting

Ascendend to Immortal

  • Having your best game every time
  • Mastering every Aspect of the game flawlessly

Immortal to Radiant

  • Doing every Aspect perfectly and mastering your Agents to the full potential and having perfect mechanics, which you can use every game
